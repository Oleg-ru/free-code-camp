Lazania

Welcome. In this recept we do it is great lazania

Ingredients

  • 600g Meat
  • 600g Balanieza
  • 60g Butter
  • 2.1/2 Flour
  • 2 Olive Oil
  • 750ml Milk
  • 10 Shit lazania
  • 500g Cheese

Instructions

  1. Put butter and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a saucepan and melt. Gradually add the flour and stir so that no lumps remain.
  2. When all the flour is mixed, pour in all the milk. Turn down the heat and simmer until the desired consistency: not liquid, but not too thick. It is important to constantly stir the sauce so that it does not burn. The consistency of low-fat sour cream.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan. Add minced meat (preferably pork + veal).
  4. Roast the minced meat until it is half cooked. Pour the bolognese sauce into it, add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees. Grease the mold with butter. Pour a little bechamel sauce on the bottom, just a little, just to cover the bottom.
  6. Lay out the layers (not boiled). Put the resulting minced meat on the layers (we do not regret it!), grated cheese on the minced meat. The cheese has bechamel sauce.
  7. Add as much sauce as you think is necessary to make the lasagna juicy. Place the dry lasagna sheets on top of the sauce. Repeat the procedure.
  8. Spread the last layer of sheets with bechamel sauce and sprinkle cheese generously on top. Let stand for 7-10 minutes. Put it in the oven. Bake for 30 minutes.
